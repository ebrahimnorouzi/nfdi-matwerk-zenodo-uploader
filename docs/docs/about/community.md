# NFDI-MatWerk Community

## What is NFDI-MatWerk?

[NFDI-MatWerk](https://nfdi-matwerk.de) is the National Research Data Infrastructure
for Materials Science and Engineering in Germany. It is part of the broader
[NFDI](https://www.nfdi.de) initiative funded by the Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft (DFG).

The consortium brings together universities, research institutes, and industry
partners to build a shared, FAIR data infrastructure for materials science.

## Zenodo community

All deposits made through this uploader are submitted to:

> **[zenodo.org/communities/nfdi-matwerk](https://zenodo.org/communities/nfdi-matwerk)**

Community membership means your dataset is:

- Discoverable by other MatWerk researchers
- Curated according to MatWerk metadata standards
- Linked to the broader NFDI data ecosystem

## FAIR data principles

MatWerk promotes [FAIR data](https://www.go-fair.org/fair-principles/) —
**Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable**. Publishing via Zenodo
with proper metadata and an ORCID directly supports all four principles.
