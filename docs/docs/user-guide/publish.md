# Step 4 – Review & Publish

## Review screen

The final step shows a summary of everything before you publish:

- **Reserved DOI** — assigned by Zenodo when the draft was created. This DOI is
  permanent once published; you can cite it in papers before the deposit is public.
- **Basic info** — title and truncated description
- **Creators** — names, affiliations, ORCIDs
- **Details** — upload type, date, license, community, keywords
- **Files** — list of all successfully uploaded files with sizes

Click **Preview draft on Zenodo** to open the Zenodo draft record in a new tab
and do a final visual check before publishing.

## Publishing

Click **Publish to MatWerk community** to submit the deposit.

!!! warning "Publishing is permanent"
    Once published, a Zenodo deposit **cannot be deleted**. The DOI is registered
    with DataCite and the record becomes part of the permanent scholarly record.
    You can upload new versions, but the original record remains.

## After publishing

A success screen shows:

- The **final DOI** as a clickable link (`https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.…`)
- A **View on Zenodo** button
- A **Start new upload** button to reset the wizard

!!! note "Community review"
    Deposits submitted to `nfdi-matwerk` may undergo a curation review by the
    community curators before appearing in the community listing. The record is
    public immediately, but community membership may take a few days.

## Discarding a draft

If you want to abandon a deposit before publishing, click **Discard draft**.
This permanently deletes the draft and all uploaded files from Zenodo's sandbox.
